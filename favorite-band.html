Biography

Maroon 5 is one of the most popular bands of the new millennium. Their album "Songs About Jane" propelled the band into mainstream. Lead singer, Adam Levine, appeared on the US singing show The Voice futhuring the success of the group. The group has won 3 Grammys, 3 American Music Awards, 3 People's Choice Awards, 4 Billboard Music Awards, and 5 Teen Choice Awards.
